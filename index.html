The Internet Terminology Encyclopedia

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to the technologies that power our modern digital world. In this project, we explore the fundamental architecture of the Internet and the World Wide Web, breaking down the complex systems that allow for global communication, data sharing, and interactive web applications.

Understanding these terms is crucial for anyone looking to build or manage digital tools. From the low-level protocols like TCP/IP that move data packets across the globe, to the high-level browsers we use to view content, each component plays a vital role in the ecosystem of the 21st century.
